Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes — Pro includes a 14-day free trial with full access. No credit card required to start.

What counts as a "search"?

Each query submitted to Logicra counts as one search, regardless of the response length or mode used.

How does annual billing work?

Annual plans are billed once per year at a 20% discount compared to monthly pricing.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes — students get 50% off Pro with a verified .edu email. Contact us to apply.

What's included in Enterprise?

Custom SLAs, dedicated infrastructure, fine-tuning, compliance certifications, and a dedicated account manager.
